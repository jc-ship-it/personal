# Prototyping with AI

AI is changing how we prototype. Not replacing the designer, but amplifying our ability to explore.

## Speed of iteration

What used to take hours can now take minutes. Generate variations, test hypotheses, iterate faster.

## Human in the loop

The best results come from combining AI generation with human judgment. AI proposes; designers decide.

## Conclusion

Embrace AI as a prototyping partner. The tools will keep evolving—our job is to stay curious.
